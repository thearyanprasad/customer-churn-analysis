# 📉 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

> **What if you could identify a customer before they leave?**

Every lost customer impacts revenue, growth, and long-term business stability. In highly competitive telecom markets, understanding **why customers churn** is often more valuable than acquiring new customers.

This project explores customer behavior across **7,032 telecom customers** and uncovers the factors driving customer attrition through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

## 🎯 The Business Problem

A telecom company is experiencing customer loss and declining revenue.

Management wants answers to three critical questions:

- Which customers are most likely to churn?
- What factors contribute most to customer attrition?
- How much revenue is being lost due to churn?

The goal was not just to visualize data, but to transform customer records into actionable business intelligence.

## 🕵️ Investigation Findings

After analyzing customer demographics, contract types, payment methods, internet services, and tenure patterns, several strong churn indicators emerged.

### Finding #1 — Contract Type Matters

Customers on **Month-to-Month contracts** churn dramatically more than customers locked into long-term agreements.

**Insight:** Contract commitment strongly influences retention.

---

### Finding #2 — The First Year Is Critical

Customer churn is highest during the early stages of the customer lifecycle.

**Insight:** Retention strategies should focus heavily on onboarding and first-year engagement.

---

### Finding #3 — Fiber Optic Customers Are Leaving

Fiber Optic users represent the largest proportion of churned customers.

**Insight:** Pricing, service quality, or customer expectations may require further investigation.

---

### Finding #4 — Payment Method Signals Risk

Customers paying via **Electronic Check** show the highest churn levels.

**Insight:** Payment behavior can serve as an early churn indicator.

---

## 🎯 Highest Risk Customer Profile

🚨 **Fiber Optic + Month-to-Month Contract + Electronic Check**

Customers matching this profile should be prioritized for retention campaigns.
